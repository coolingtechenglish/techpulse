# Instagram April 2026 — Detailed Visual/Design Prompts

## Global Design System

### Brand Identity
- **Primary palette:** Dark Navy `#1a1a2e` / Electric Blue `#00d4ff` / White `#ffffff`
- **Accent palette:** Coral `#ff6b6b` (for ❌ wrong examples), Green `#4ecdc4` (for ✅ correct examples), Soft Yellow `#ffe66d` (for 💡 tips)
- **Typography:** Inter Bold for English headings, Noto Sans TC Bold for Chinese headings, Inter Regular / Noto Sans TC Regular for body text
- **Instagram canvas:** 1080 × 1080 px (square) for single images, 1080 × 1350 px (4:5 portrait) for carousels
- **Consistent elements:** Rounded corners (16px radius) on content cards, subtle drop shadows, 60px safe margin on all edges

### Slide Templates (for Carousels)
- **Cover slide:** Full dark navy background, large bold title in white/electric blue, small subtitle in Chinese, decorative icon/emoji cluster in corner
- **Content slide:** Dark navy background, content card (white or semi-transparent) centered, bilingual text layout
- **CTA/Closing slide:** Gradient background (navy → electric blue), motivational closing line, "Follow for more" + account handle
